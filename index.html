Visit "Steel City" Pittsburgh

Enjoy our culture, sports, and beautiful scenery. Home of the Pittsburgh Steelers, Penguins, and Pirates.

Top three activities to do in Pittsburgh

Atttend a museum

Andy Warhol Museum is the largest in North America dedicated to a single artist.

Enjoy international cuisine and fresh produce

The Strip District is a lively and historic neighborhood known for its unique shops, food markets, and eclectic street vendors.

See stunning outdoor and indoor gardens

Phipps Conservatory and Botanical Gardens features stunning indoor and outdoor gardens with a wide variety of exotic plants, flowers, and themed exhibits.

Fun facts about Pittsburgh

Pittsburgh is often referred to as the "City of Bridges" because it has a remarkable 446 bridges within its city limits.

In 1896, the first public screening of a movie in the United States took place in Pittsburgh.

The beloved children's television program, "Mister Rogers' Neighborhood," was filmed in Pittsburgh
